ABOUT

Chennai is a culture-rich city with carnatic music, delicious food, serene beaches and popular temples. It is one of the most preferred tourist destinations in India.

Chennai, originally known as Madras Patnam, was located in the province of Tondaimandalam, an area lying between Pennar river of Nellore and the Pennar river of Cuddalore.

Chennai is the capital city of Tamilnadu. It is one of the most visited tourist locations in India. Many temples, old historical buildings add to the beauty of the city. Chennai is the “Gateway to South India”. Every Indian has a reason to love this place because it has not only embraced people coming from diverse backgrounds, it has also uplifted and moulded their culture with great respect and care. Chennai holds some of the most soul-stirring and sensational cultural events
